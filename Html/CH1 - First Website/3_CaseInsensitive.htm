h1 and H1 are same in HTML

h1 and H1 are same in HTML. It is case insensitive. So, h1, H1, h2, H2, h3, H3, h4, H4, h5, H5, h6 and H6 are all same in HTML. They are all heading tags. The only difference is that they have different default font sizes. h1 is the largest and h6 is the smallest.`
